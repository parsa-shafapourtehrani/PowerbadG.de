about us

PowerBad GmbH is a medium-sized company characterized by solid craftsmanship and reliable order processing. Our dedicated team has extensive expertise and many years of professional experience, which we bring to every project. Our core competencies lie in modern sanitary technology - from new builds to solutions suitable for the elderly and disabled. We also offer highly efficient cooling and heating technology, including air conditioning systems, condensing boiler technology, solid fuel boilers, oil heating systems, heat pumps and the optimization of central heating systems. Our range of services is complemented by advanced solar technologies. With currently 14 employees, we concentrate on the project planning and implementation of complete renovations as well as the creation, maintenance, expansion and optimization of larger heating systems, including solar and heat pump technology. Rely on PowerBad GmbH for tailor-made solutions that combine efficiency, innovation and sustainability.
